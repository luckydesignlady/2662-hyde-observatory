Interested in Astronomy?

Check out the Prarie Astronomy Club. The club meets at Hyde Observatory on the last Tuesday of every month at 7:30pm.

Things to do this month

January Program #1: "POINT OF NO RETURN"

Describes the exotic science of quasars an super massive black holes. Running time: 20 minutes

January Program #2: "The Winter Constellations"

A tour of the evening sky above Lincoln, Nebraska during the frigid winter months: Perfect viewing weather when it's not snowing. Running time: 20 minutes.

Location

Hyde Observatory can be found at 3701 S 70th St, Lincoln, NE 68506

Hours

Hyde is open every Saturday night year round. We may be closed if Saturday falls on or very near a major holiday. If in doubt, call first.

April - September 8PM to 11PM

October - March 7PM to 10PM
